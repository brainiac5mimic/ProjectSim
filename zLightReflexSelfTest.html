Photic Sneeze Reflex: Self Test

 

How to test yourself for photic sneeze reflex:

        1). Do the experiment during the day when the sun is overhead and clearly visible.

        2). Go indoors into a dark room, preferably with the lights off, no bright windows, and no devices. Stay in the room for approximately 10-20 min.

        3). Prompty walk outside and look up to the sun for about 30 seconds, but not directly at it (as that could damage your eyes).

        4). Repetition of this test will produce the most accurate result, but it is safe to assume that if you sneezed during step three you have the photic sneeze reflex, and if you did not sneeze, you probably do not have the reflex.
